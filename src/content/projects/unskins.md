[unturned](https://store.steampowered.com/app/304930/Unturned/) has mass-loyal players and zero infrastructure. no one ever built a proper skin trading platform for it. so i did.

unskins is an automated web portal where you deposit your old skins and withdraw new ones from the site's bot. no DMs, no trust trades, no "you go first." just a swap.

the funniest part - people started showing up before i finished building it. no marketing, no launch post, nothing. turns out if you build the _only thing that exists_ for a community that desperately needs it, they'll find you.

still adding features. still the only one doing this.
